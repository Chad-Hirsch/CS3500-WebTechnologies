The Team 5 Guitar Touch

It’s no secret that Team 5 Guitars have a unique look, feel, and sound. What may appear to simply be aesthetically innovative design is actually a triumph of science and sound engineering. Creating purpose-built sound is no easy task. That’s why, early on, Team 5 re-engineered the acoustic guitar by re-envisioning how each and every component contributes to the overall sound. It’s what goes into a Team 5 Guitar that truly delivers unparalleled, distinctively crafted sound. As you browse our guitar series, you’ll find examples of purpose built instruments designed to deliver your signature sound.

Services Overview

Custom Jazz 13 Archtop Guitar

This Jazz-13 is the most versatile and smallest archtop produced by Team 5 Guitars. The smaller body is very light weight and helps reduce feedback, but still gives a fat warm, Jazz tone. All carved solid woods give great clarity and chordal note separation and the ft woody warm, dark, tone found in great carved solid archtops.

Acoustic A-Series

Designed to be the ideal performance acoustic guitar, the A-Series is crafted from the ground up to be perfectly suited to life on the road. It can be played-in comfort out of the box thanks to hand-rolled fingerboard edges on a straight taper neck. The A-Series is the perfect guitar to take your playing to the next level.

Signature Series

Our Signature series acoustics are built with the highest quality woods, materials and components possible. Working alongside industry leaders to specify high end transducers and preamps, our attention to detail across all the models give the Signature Series guitars a special quality all their own.
