Electric Guitars

The Electric Guitar Meets Your Style

Team 5 Guitars believes your instrument should be as unique as the music you make with it. That’s why we build custom electric guitars. Choose your guitar’s shape, color, parts and pickups and even personalize it with custom text or graphics.
Our team of dedicated guitar enthusiasts, musicians and luthiers build the guitars at our shop in Kalamazoo, MI and ship them to your door.

Style Overview

Diamond Series

We got a little jealous watching you have all the fun designing our guitars…

The Diamond Series is a collection of guitars designed by the team at Team 5 Guitars. These instruments are built by our luthiers in Kalamazoo Michigan in small batches. Each instrument in the Diamond Series features unique styles, materials and components that are not available in our Custom Series.

ACRYLIC TOP

Over the Ribs chambered body design we inlay a colored acrylic faceplate. This acts like a large pickguard while also serving as a stiff reflective top that works with the wood to create resonance.

MORE TONE, LESS WEIGHT

All our guitars are special in large part due to the unique chambered body design.
These maple bodies have our “Rival Ribs” chambering pattern for improved tone and reduced weight.
